About Me

I worked as a media designer (digital, print, av) since 2012. In 2014 I produced and published two episodes of the audioplay "Windur, der Wundersucher" for kids.

Through content creation I also got in touch with social media marketing while working for save.co, weinundbar.de and gameswelt.de.

For a long while gastronomy was my part-time passion. I started at the bar, in the kitchen and in service, going on as shift supervisor and marketing assistant and ended up in event managment and as a gastronomer.

Due to the pandemic I dedided for a reboot and began to teach myself web development in 2021. Actually I'm participating in a web development bootcamp at neuefische.de.
